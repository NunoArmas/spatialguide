Introduction


Tourism is very important area of business. It stimulates the local economy and contributes positively to culturally enrich every tourists and visitors. Currently, there are multiple services that offer tour guides to a city/region, showing the historical background of the different tour locations. Lots of these services are automatic, as they offer disposable headphones for the tour guide, playing sound according to the location they are visiting.

But... what if the sound actually comes from the location itself? Wouldn't be a greater and better experience if the sound is coming from the location, instead of having the same explination "at your right you have..."?

SpatialGuide is an app whose goal is to provide a immersive experience to tourists and travel enthusiasts, using audio spatialization (3D Audio) technology to make users hear sound coming from certain spots and locations. This app will also allow tour companies to create tour routes, setting up points of interest and assign them audio files that will play according to the user's location, when approaching the assigned spot.

System Features


These are the main features of SpatialGuide:

- Use GPS position to track user position;
- Use the head position to track the user's position related to the point of interest (using InertiaCube);
- Plays a previously defined audio (file) using audio spatialization when the user approaches a point of interest defined in the tour route (Trigger Area);
- Create, modify and delete new points of interest and new tour routes (set of points of interest);
- Store of tour routes in a database;
- Download of tour routes for offline usage;
- Audio spatialization of the audio files to give feel that the sound comes from a given source and direction.



The Architecture





The app in the smartphone will gather Route data from the administrator dashboard via internet, user's head orientation from the Head tracking device via bluetooth and position from the smartphone's GPS.
With this combined information, the app will produce a stereo sound with the explanation of the local.

The App


To use the SpatialGuide app, one should register an account. Upon registration, user can enter the system by inserting their credentials to sign in the app:


After entering in the system through the app, according to the city, the user can select one of the available tour routes for downloading, or insert a code for a specific route.
Then, after the selecting the route, it's shown on the screen the user current location.


When the user enters near a route point of interest, it is played an sound about that point of interest and the app shows on screen some information and details about the given point of interest.It is also possible to visualize written information of that point of interest in the screen.


The user can also customize the app according to its preferences in the app menu. Currently it is only possible to view the map and contact us. More features will come soon
