Features

The main features of our System

SpatialGuide features:


- GPS position to track user position;
- Registers head position related to the point of interest;
- Stores the information of users, and points of interest in a database;
- Register to store user information in order to provide a more customised experience;
- Create, modify and delete new points of interest and new tour routes and save the audio explanation on the database;
- Plays an audio explanation whenever the use approaches a point of interest;
- Auralization engine integrated with head tracking to envolve the user in an immersive experience;
- Download of tour routes for offline usage;
- Route visualization in real time to track your position and marks the next point of interest
